About The Sitters


Most of us have critters and need help with feedings, playtime, and walkies, and you can find a current list of all your local pet sitters who are ready to chip in.

This is all done by volunteers who love animals, and we just ask that you add yourself to the roster so you can take a turn helping out a furry neighbor. Walkers are ALWAYS needed!

From hampsters to scales, you'll find someone who'll love your pet reguardles of their species.
